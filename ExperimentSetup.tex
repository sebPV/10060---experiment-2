\section{Experiment setup}
{\color{red}\Huge beskrivelse af forsøget incl håndtegnet biled af opstilingen}
We have modeled an experiment to determine the relation between length and time. We placed the iOLab on an inclined plane to create constant acceleration and performed 50 tests. We performed the tests by letting the iOLab roll down the inclined plane and measuring the place, velocity, acceleration and time. We would do around five measurements before changing the angle of the incline, such that we would get different accelerations. We decided to record a shortly after letting go, so that the device had a little starting velocity. We recorded data and performed data analysis. The unevenness of plane created noise in our data.
